    How to add task or item?

    To add a task or item, either press Enter on your keyboard or click on the plus sign located next to the input field.

    How to remove task or item?

    To remove a task or item, click on the task or item you want to remove, and then double-click on the times (cross) sign.

    How to add a task or item as Completed or Purchased?

    Double-click on the task or item to mark it as Completed or Purchased.

    How to remove a task or item as Completed or Purchased?

    Double-click on the task or item to mark it as Incomplete or Remaining.

    What is delete tasks or items?

    When you click on it, three options are shown: Delete All, Delete Completed or Purchased, and Delete Incomplete or Remaining.

    • The first option deletes all tasks or items.
    • The second option deletes all completed(purchased) tasks(items).
    • The third option deletes all incomplete(remaining) tasks(items).
    What is Multi-Select?

    It deletes all selected tasks or items.

    What is Advanced Mode?

    It is a feature that makes this to-do list more useful. When enabled, the to-do list is converted into a shopping list, allowing you to add items with their quantities and prices. You can also use it for many other purposes.

    What is Calculator Mode?

    It is a feature that works with advanced mode. It takes the values of quantity and prices from advanced mode, calculates the total, and displays it. To use it, you must enter values in advanced mode.

    What is Budget Mode?

    This feature is enabled when both advanced mode and calculator mode are on. It helps you to manage your budget by warning you if the total price of your items exceeds your budget. To use this feature properly, you must enter both the price and quantity of the items.

    Full Form of units

    This are Full Form of units you see when advanced mode is enabled

    • kg - kilogram
    • g - gram
    • L - Liter
    • ml - mililiter
    • pc - piece
    • st - set
    • dz - dozen
    • /kg - per kilogram
    • /g - per gram
    • /L - per Liter
    • /ml - per mililiter
    • /pc - per piece
    • /st - per set
    • /dz - per dozen
    More about units

    In advanced mode, you have options for units like kg and per kg. If you know the price per kg, you can enter it by selecting "/kg" in the price field. If you don`t know the per kg price but know the total price, you can enter it by selecting "kg" in the price field. Units like gram and milliliter are represented by default as "g" and "ml" in the price field. If you want to add a price per milliliter or per gram, you can select "/g" or "/ml" in the price field. If you choose "g" in the quantity field, by default it will display "g" in the price field.

    What is Mrp Total?

    This feature is enabled when calculator mode is on. To use it properly, you must enter the values of quantity and prices for the items you have entered.

    What does Show Mrp does?

    When clicked, it shows the total of purchased items in the "Purchased" section, the total of remaining items in the "Remaining" section, and the total of all items in the "All" section.

    What does Mrp Multi-Select Do?

    When you click on it, you can select items. After selecting items, click on Done to see the total of selected items in the "Selected" section, the total of not selected items in the "Not Selected" section, and the total of all items in the "All" section.
